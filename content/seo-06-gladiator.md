## Description

Almost everything you know about Roman gladiators is wrong.

They weren't all slaves fighting to the death. The real gladiators were professional athletes — celebrities with fan graffiti, a mostly vegetarian "barley men" diet, and surprisingly good odds of survival (roughly 19 in 20). The famous "we who are about to die salute you" was never their line, and the thumbs-down death signal comes from a Victorian painting, not from Rome. This is what life as a gladiator was really like — beyond the Hollywood myth.
